About us

OCertainly, let's remove the mention of competitors and other details: Our business story began nearly 40 years ago near Kandy. Initially, we were a small bakery focusing on baking essentials like bread and buns. Our primary customers were local residents and students, drawn to our bakery because of our reasonable prices. As our reputation for delicious treats spread, more people started purchasing from us. As we experienced success, we expanded our operations and eventually established a large bakery. By 1995, we had grown to operate seven successful businesses. Over time, we diversified our menu to offer a variety of food items, catering to different tastes. In the year 2000, we faced new challenges, prompting us to explore new opportunities. This led us to venture into the world of fast food. Our efforts were fruitful, and today we proudly offer a diverse range of food options. Recognizing the shift in consumer habits, particularly the widespread use of phones and the internet, we are leveraging technology to connect with a broader audience. Currently, we are working on a website to simplify the process of ordering food online. Our goal is to make our delicious offerings easily accessible to more customers and continue the growth of our business.

Mission

At Breezy Bite Bakery, our goal is to make our customers happy with yummy baked goods and tasty fast food. We want to be a special part of our community by offering great products and creating a friendly vibe in all seven of our branches. We work hard to always come up with new and delicious things, aiming to go above and beyond what our customers expect and be known as a local favorite.

Vision

We dream of Breezy Bite Bakery being known as a top name in the food world, where we set the bar for quality and making customers happy. We want to keep growing and stay connected to our roots in Kandy. Embracing the digital age, we imagine a website that makes ordering easy and shows off our history and commitment to being the best. As leaders, we promise to keep up with what people like and make sure Breezy Bite Bakery continues to mean tasty food for many years.
