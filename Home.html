Where every bite tells a story

Welcome to Breezy Bite Bakery, a culinary journey that began nearly 40 years ago near Kandy. Starting as a small bakery focused on essentials like bread, we've grown into a beloved establishment offering a diverse menu, including delicious fast food. Committed to our community, we're expanding our reach online to make ordering our delightful treats easier. At Breezy Bite Bakery, we aim to bring joy through our baked goods and fast food, crafting a friendly atmosphere in all seven branches. Join us as we continue to grow, innovate, and bring the best of tasty traditions to your table.
